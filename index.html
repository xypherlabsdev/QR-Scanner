Privacy Policy

QRScan Pro — Generate · Scan · Manage

1 Introduction

Welcome to QRScan Pro ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency in how we handle your data.

By using QRScan Pro, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the app.

2 Information We Collect

Camera Access: We access your device camera solely for scanning QR codes and barcodes. Camera data is processed locally on your device and is never transmitted to external servers.

Gallery/Photo Access: We access your gallery only when you choose to scan a QR code from an existing image. This data remains on your device.

Scan History: QR codes you scan are stored locally on your device in an encrypted database. This data is never uploaded to our servers.

User Preferences: App settings such as theme, language, sound, and vibration preferences are stored locally on your device.

Analytics Data: We use Firebase Analytics to collect anonymized usage data, including:

  • App usage patterns and feature interactions
  • Device type, operating system version, and app version
  • Crash reports and performance metrics
  • General geographic region (country level)

3 How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain QR code scanning and generation functionality
  • To store your scan history for quick access and reference
  • To remember your app preferences and settings
  • To analyze app performance, identify bugs, and improve user experience

4 Data Storage & Security

Your scan history and preferences are stored locally on your device using encrypted databases and secure storage mechanisms. We do not store personal data on external servers.

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information. However, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5 Data Retention & Deletion

All locally stored data (scan history, preferences) can be deleted at any time through the app's Settings. You can also clear all data by uninstalling the app.

Anonymized analytics data collected by Firebase is retained according to Google's data retention policies.

6 Children's Privacy

QRScan Pro is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will take steps to remove such data.

7 Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal data
  • Withdraw consent for data collection
  • Request a copy of data collected about you
  • Lodge a complaint with a data protection authority
